## 4. Examples
### Example 1:
Find the derivative of $f(x) = x^3$.

**Solution:**
$$
f'(x) = \frac{d}{dx}(x^3) = 3x^2
$$

### Example 2:
Evaluate:
$$
\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx
$$

**Solution:**
$$
\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \left[ \frac{x^3}{3} \right]_0^1 = \frac{1}{3}
$$
